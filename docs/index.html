About Me

Hey! I'm Alejandro Sobko, a software developer based in Argentina. I work remotely at Altitude-Sports, mainly focused on frontend development with Next.js.

Over the years, I've worked on a variety of projects in different roles—both as part of a team and as a freelancer. I also had the chance to teach ReactJS at EducaciónIT, which I really enjoyed because I love sharing what I learn.

Even though I'm currently more focused on frontend, I also have backend experience using technologies like Java, Rails, and Django. I'm especially interested in building smooth user experiences, and from time to time I like digging into web app vulnerabilities and learning more about security.

Outside of work, I enjoy reading, spending time outdoors, and having mate with my family. One of my favorite side projects is Tikijuegos, a game I built for my son using React and drag & drop. You can check that out (and more) on my GitHub, or learn more about my experience on LinkedIn.
